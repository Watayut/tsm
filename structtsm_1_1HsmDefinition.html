Detailed Description

template<typename HsmDef>
struct tsm::HsmDefinition< HsmDef >

Captures 'structural' aspects of the state machine and behavior specific to HsmDef. For e.g. the HsmDef can override the onEntry and onExit behaviors to implement history preserving policy for specific events.
